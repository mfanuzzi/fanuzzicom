  • Lazerbeams Tonite

    Musician, Producer, Creative Technologist, Engineer, Art Director...

    LB:2NITE is an ongoing collaborative art project between meself and me mate Axton Frick. What began as simply improvising some phat beats turned into a multimedia, son et lumière journey of connection (audience-performer), sounds like an abstraction of 1,000 genres, and aims to push experiential boundaries by integrating biofeedback, emotional design, and massive mingling of modalities. To do this, I've built a custom recording/performance system in my studio, that enables live layering and manipulation of both electric and acoustic instruments, FFT drum triggering and algorithmic transposition, all the while capturing the performance in a traditional DAW (REAPER). Simultaneously, various biofeedback sensors combine with synesthetic interpretations of the sounds (via Max and VVVV) to visualize the experience for projection and light manipulation. These are only a few of the technologies behind this project, but this project is not about technology. It is about transcendence. It is an exciting journey to be on, and I hope you get to be a part of it one day.
